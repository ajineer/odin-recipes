Description

  • turkey
  • bread
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • onion
  • pepper jack cheese

Directions

  1. Lightly toast bread in toaster oven at 350 degrees
  2. heat up turkey and place 3-5 slices on bread slices
  3. slice tomato and onion however you wish
  4. add lettuce, tomato and onion to top of turkey
  5. add pepper jack cheese to top, let sandwhich stand in toaster oven for cheese to melt
